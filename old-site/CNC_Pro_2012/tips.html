Our company, CNC-Pro Ltd. is seated in Veresegyház, Hungary – about 25 kms away from capital, Budapest.
 
Our staff has gained 10-15 years of technical experience at leading multinational companies.
 
Main scopes of activities:
  • Installation and repair of regular, NC and CNC machine tools
  • Planning and execution of preventive maintenance activities
  • Extensive and complete mechanical and/or electrical overhaul, bearing exchange on main spindles, controller replacement/enhancement

The international co-operations we have developed during the years provide us that we can quickly source any kinds CNC spare parts.

Types of machines within our scope:

  • milling machines
  • horizontal and vertical lathes
  • sawing machines
  • stamping machines
  • sheet cutting equipment, press brakes and pipe benders
  • water-jet cutting machines

The continuous growth and development are really important for our company. Therefore we regularly participate on technical trainings at respected European machine tool building companies – e.g. in Poland, Slovakia, Italy, Switzerland and The Netherlands.

Our young(-hearted) team is seeking challenges – we are thirsty to combine that with our knowledge and experience in order to prove that for more and more satisfied Partners.
